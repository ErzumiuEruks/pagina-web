About us

  • Nike is one of the world’s largest and most recognized brands in sportswear and footwear, known for its innovation, performance, and remarkable design. Founded in 1964, the company pioneered the creation of products that support athletes' performance and inspire individuals to push new boundaries. With a wide range of products for various sports and activities, Nike blends advanced technology with modern aesthetics, offering items that not only enhance performance but also fit an active and dynamic lifestyle. Nike’s commitment to sustainability and innovation continues to define the brand, and its campaigns inspire millions globally to dream big and exceed their limits.

History

  • Nike was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports. Initially, it served as a distributor for the Japanese brand Onitsuka Tiger before launching its own line of footwear in 1971. The company rebranded as Nike, named after the Greek goddess of victory, and introduced the iconic Swoosh logo. Nike quickly grew into a global leader in sportswear, revolutionizing athletic performance with innovative designs and technologies. Over time, the brand expanded into apparel, accessories, and equipment, becoming a symbol of athletic excellence and fashion worldwide.

Locations

  • Nike has a global presence, with headquarters located in Beaverton, Oregon, USA. The company operates numerous offices, retail stores, and distribution centers worldwide, ensuring its products are accessible in over 190 countries. Nike’s flagship locations are often found in major cities such as New York, London, Tokyo, and Shanghai, showcasing the brand's innovation and cutting-edge designs. In addition to its physical stores, Nike has a strong online presence, offering a seamless shopping experience for customers globally. The company’s global network enables it to reach athletes and consumers in diverse markets, promoting a lifestyle of performance and inspiration.
